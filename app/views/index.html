The Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA) carries out disease surveillance to identify new and re-emerging diseases in farmed animals in England and Wales.

This price list shows subsidised prices you can use when you submit samples to APHA laboratories as part of this work.

If your submission does not meet these conditions, use one of the price lists below.

Before you submit a sample for testing

Read guidance on how to select samples and choose appropriate tests.

How to submit your sample

You can use the animal disease testing service to create submissions and check your results online for most tests.

Read guidance on:

  • submitting samples from farm animals for diagnostic testing
  • submitting farm animals for a post-mortem examination

Other price lists

APHA test fees for export, captive avian import and ABC apply to tests for:

  • exporting animals and live animal products
  • importing captive avians – statutory tests required under EU directives
  • carrying out animal breeding control

APHA Scientific offers a range of commercial tests for:

  • submissions which are not eligible for disease surveillance testing
  • samples from companion animals
  • animals outside England and Wales at the time of sampling
